✨ Features at a Glance
🔍
Multi-keyword Search
Search for multiple terms at once using commas. We'll grab everything and automatically remove any duplicates.
📊
Interactive Analytics
Click on any chart or map to filter the table instantly. You can mix and match visual filters with dropdowns seamlessly.
🎛️
Advanced Filtering
Drill down using powerful dropdowns: Study Type, Phase, Primary Purpose, Intervention, Allocation, Masking, Sex, and Results.
⚙️
Customize View
Control exactly what details appear when you expand a trial row by toggling specific sections on or off to reduce clutter.
📋
Trial Detail Expand
Click any row to reveal in-depth trial details, including criteria, outcomes, contacts, locations, and clickable PubMed links.
📄
Batch PDF Export
Check the boxes next to studies you like, then click 'PDF' to download a clean, multi-page report of your selections.
⬇️
CSV Bulk Export
Click 'CSV' to download your filtered list to a spreadsheet. Select specific rows to export only those.
🔢
Duplicate Detection
If a trial matches more than one of your search terms, we flag it. Click 'Duplicates' at the top to see them.

Recruitment Status Guide
  • Not yet recruiting — Study has not started recruiting yet.
  • Recruiting — Currently recruiting participants.
  • Enrolling by invitation — Only pre-selected participants from a specific population are invited.
  • Active, not recruiting — Ongoing study; participants are being treated but no new enrollment.
  • Suspended — Stopped early but may restart.
  • Terminated — Stopped early and will not restart.
  • Completed — Ended normally; last participant's last visit has occurred.
  • Withdrawn — Stopped before enrolling its first participant.
  • Unknown — Status not verified within the past 2 years past its completion date.
